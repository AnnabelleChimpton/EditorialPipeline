# SITUATION DOSSIER TEMPLATE

## Event

## Confidence
- confirmed / developing / unverified

## Urgency
- critical / high / standard

## Timeline of developments
- (Chronological, timestamped entries. Each entry: time, what happened, source attribution.)

## Background & context
- (What led here? Prior agreements, recent escalation patterns, relevant history. Keep tight — only context that directly informs this event.)

## Key actors & positions
- (Named individuals and organizations. Official titles. What each party has said or done, attributed.)

## Humanitarian / civilian dimension
- (Civilian impact: casualties, displacement, infrastructure damage, aid access. Sourced to UN agencies, ICRC, or named reporters. If no data available, say so.)

## International response
- (Statements, votes, sanctions, diplomatic moves by external actors. Attributed and timestamped.)

## Analysis: what this means
- (What does this development change? What are the implications for the conflict trajectory? Keep analytical, not speculative. Ground in facts from the timeline.)

## What to watch
- (Forward-looking: what developments would confirm, escalate, or resolve this situation? What decisions are pending?)

## Sources
- (5-8 sources, with outlet name, publication timestamp, and URL)
